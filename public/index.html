About Us

Navro Exports excels as a reputed manufacturer and exporter of Knitted Under Garments for Mens, Boys, Ladies and Girls. The company is based in Noida and has its presence felt in the global production and export world of under garment manufacturing in a very short time.

Our main strengths include:

Technology.

We have state of the art manufacturing machinery including the PZ-100, AD-2010 and FFGG.

Design.

We have a team of world class designers and tailors using the best available designing systems.

Teamwork.

We have highly qualified and committed personnel who are committed to ensuring that all our product are of the highest quality.

Delivery.

We spare no expense or efforts to deliver as promised and give our customers the best price, highest quality and timely delivery.
